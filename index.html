We don't want
your data.

Genuinely. Most apps say that and then bury the truth in a 40-page privacy policy nobody reads. So here it is in plain English — no small print, no legal gymnastics, no hoping you don't ask.

📱 It lives on your phone, not our servers
Your taste profile, your swipes, your preferences — all stored locally on your device. We don't have a big database of your Friday nights somewhere. We literally can't look at it, even if we wanted to. Which we don't.
🚫 We don't sell your data. Full stop.
Not to advertisers, not to "partners", not to anyone. No third-party data brokers, no quietly selling anonymised profiles, none of that. Your nights out are nobody else's business.
🤝 We ask. You decide.
When we need something — your location, your dietary stuff, your vibes — we ask permission first. Clearly. No pre-ticked boxes, no "by continuing you agree to..." buried in the terms. You say yes or you say no. Either way, the app still works.
📣 Yes, the free version has ads
We have to make money somehow, and we'd rather show you an ad for a cocktail bar in the Northern Quarter than charge you a tenner a month. The ads are contextual — based on what you're doing in the app, not what you googled last Tuesday. Want them gone? There's an ad-free version. No judgement either way.
🗑️ Delete means delete
If you want out, you're out. One tap and your data is gone — properly gone, not "deleted" while we quietly keep a backup for eighteen months. No guilt trips, no "are you sure?" dark patterns. It's your data. Always was.
